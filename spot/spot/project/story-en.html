SPOT EDITORIAL · MANIFESTO
CHAPTER · 01

The city is half-empty,
yet hard to drop into.

Ground-floor vacancy in Seoul is 9.5%. In some districts, 14.7%. A storefront sits dark for three, sometimes six months. While it does, the alley loses a little of its pulse.

Meanwhile, an Instagram-born brand is looking for its first physical popup. A year-long lease is too much. A KakaoTalk reply from a landlord never comes. The spaces exist; the people exist. The meeting is what's missing.

CHAPTER · 02 / A

What we
don't build.

  • Payments · escrow · PG
  • E-contracts · signatures
  • In-app chat · messaging
  • Dispute mediation
  • Holding deposits

Legal weight sits with the two parties. We ship a standard contract template, but the deal itself happens between you. Matching alone is hard enough.

CHAPTER · 02 / B

What we
do build.

  • Search — by town, date, budget
  • Listing — 5 photos, one-line intro
  • Requests — tenant → host, 1:1
  • Accept — only then do contacts open
  • Verification — photo and on-site

Two strangers meeting safely. After that, we trust your judgment to take it from there.

CHAPTER · 04 · THREE PRINCIPLES

Three questions we keep asking.

01/

Stay light.
Build less.

If something needs two screens when one would do, we won't build it. A landlord on a phone should finish listing in under three minutes.

02/

The deal is yours.

Once you're matched, we step back. We don't handle payments, contracts, or disputes. We get you to the meeting; the rest is yours.

03/

Read by town,
not by listing.

Ground floors live in neighborhoods. Seongsu's energy, Buam's quiet, Mangwon's value. We index the city town by town so listings make sense.

CHAPTER · 05 / THE TEAM

Four people, Seoul.

Spot is a small team in Seoul: design, engineering, operations — four of us. We started in May 2026 in the first five towns: Seongsu, Yeonnam, Mangwon, Buam, Hannam.

We don't build payments, contracts, in-app chat, or dispute resolution. Instead we stay with one problem — getting an empty ground floor and a short stay together — and try to do that part well. Small, often revised, intentionally limited.

If you have thoughts, write to KakaoTalk @spot.kr or hello@spot.kr. Every message is read by a person.
