FAQ · BEFORE YOU LIST

Before you
get started.

If you need more answers, send us a message on KakaoTalk @spot.kr — we reply within a business day.

Can anyone see my exact address?

Only logged-in tenants see the exact street address. Visitors see the dong (neighborhood) only, with the pin offset by up to 100m near the nearest subway station.

Can I decline tenants I don't like?

Yes. Every request requires your Accept before contact info opens. You don't need to give a reason. If you ignore it, it auto-expires after 7 days.

Can I take my listing down?

Anytime. "Pause listing" removes you from search instantly. Active requests can be wrapped up or carried forward — your call.

How does photo verification work?

Within 2–3 business days of listing, our ops team reviews your photos. A clear exterior and interior earn ✓ Photo verified. If we visit in person and confirm, you get ✓✓ Site visited. Verification is free.

Where do I get a contract?

Download Spot's standard short-term lease as a PDF. Print, sign, exchange copies. Spot is not a party to the contract.
