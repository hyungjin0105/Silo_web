SECTION · 01 / TERMS OF SERVICE

Terms of service.

Spot is an information-matching service that connects empty ground-floor commercial spaces with parties seeking short-term tenancy. These terms apply to all members — both hosts and tenants.

1.1 Scope of service

Spot provides listing, search, request, acceptance, and contact-info release. Payment, contract execution, in-app chat, and dispute mediation are not provided. All dealings after acceptance occur directly between host and tenant.

1.2 Eligibility

  • Individuals or businesses 19 years or older
  • Phone-number verification with personal mobile
  • Hosts must own or have legal right to lease the listed space

1.3 Prohibited conduct

  • False listings (non-existent spaces, photos lifted from elsewhere)
  • Duplicate accounts · automation · search-result manipulation
  • Off-platform circumvention designed to avoid match fees
  • Illegal businesses (unlicensed food service · gambling · adult venues, etc.)

1.4 Changes to terms

These terms may be updated with at least 7 days' notice before the effective date. Members who do not accept changes may close their account.

SECTION · 02 / FEES & PAYMENT

Fees · payment.

Spot does not charge for listing, visibility, search, or verification. A defined match fee applies only when a deal closes.

2.1 Match fee defined

The "match fee" applies when a host accepts a tenant request and the two close a deal. It is billed to the host by default, and may be split with the tenant by mutual agreement.

2.2 Billing

Hosts confirm a match within 7 days of acceptance. The fee is billed on the 1st of the following month. Auto-charge to card or bank transfer is available.

⚠ NO CIRCUMVENTION

Matching on Spot and then completing the deal off-platform to avoid the match fee is a violation of §1.3 and grounds for account suspension. No fee is charged when a match is declined, ignored, or doesn't close.

SECTION · 03 / CANCELLATION & REFUND

Cancellation · refund.

Spot does not hold rent or deposits. Cancellations and refunds follow the policy the two parties agree on in advance. The standard contract §4 offers a recommended policy.

3.1 Recommended refund schedule (Standard Contract §4)

  • 14+ days before start — 100% refund
  • 7–13 days before — 50%
  • 3–6 days before — 30%
  • 2 days before · same-day — no refund

3.2 Host-caused cancellation

If the tenant cannot use the space due to host fault (poor condition, unilateral cancellation, etc.), the host owes a full refund and direct damages. Spot refunds any match fee already billed.

3.3 Force majeure

Natural disaster, government order, and other no-fault events are handled by mutual agreement or under §5 of the standard contract.

SECTION · 04 / PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy policy.

Spot complies with Korea's Personal Information Protection Act and the Network Act. Categories collected, retention periods, and third-party sharing are outlined below.

4.1 Data collected

  • Required — name, mobile number, email
  • Hosts also — exact listing address, business registration number (optional)
  • Tenants also — preferred towns, request history
  • Automatic — IP, cookies, device info

4.2 Retention

Deleted immediately on account closure. Records required by law (e-commerce — 5 years) are stored separately and disposed of at the end of the retention period.

4.3 Third-party disclosure

Tenant contact info is released to the host once, when the host accepts a request. No disclosure occurs before acceptance. We do not sell or otherwise share personal data.

4.4 Processors

  • Identity verification — NICE Information Service
  • KakaoTalk notifications — Kakao (NHN Cloud)
  • Infrastructure — AWS Seoul region
SECTION · 05 / OPERATIONS

Operations policy.

5.1 Verification

Listings are reviewed by our ops team before they go live. We check photo authenticity, address reality, and business-type legality within 2–3 business days. Listings that pass receive a "✓ Photo verified" badge; those visited in person receive "✓✓ Site visited".

5.2 Response expectations

Hosts are expected to respond to requests within 7 days. Hosts whose non-response rate exceeds 50% are auto-deprioritized in search. Accounts go dormant after 30 days of inactivity.

5.3 Reporting and ops intervention

Report false listings, suspected fraud, or terms violations via KakaoTalk @spot.kr or hello@spot.kr. Our ops team reviews within a business day and may issue warnings, suspend visibility, or suspend the account.

5.4 KakaoTalk notifications

New requests, acceptances, declines, and expirations are pushed instantly via KakaoTalk. Consent is given at signup; individual categories can be toggled off in account settings.

SECTION · 06 / DISPUTES

Disputes.

Per §1.1, Spot does not mediate or adjudicate disputes between host and tenant. Resolution is between the parties.

6.1 Recommended escalation

  1. Direct conversation first — KakaoTalk, phone, in person
  2. Standard contract §6 — mutual consultation
  3. External mediation — Korea Fair Trade Mediation Agency, Korean Commercial Arbitration Board
  4. Civil litigation — district court of the listing's jurisdiction
ⓘ WHAT SPOT WILL HELP WITH

On request we provide objective records — listing creation time, edit history, request and acceptance timestamps. We do not pass judgment on who is right.
